About Us

The Shezzy's Cuisin was founded in Multan by Mr. Shahzad Hussain

Shezzy's cuisine, though limited in its offerings to pizza, burger, and samosa, has managed to carve out a niche for itself by perfecting these beloved and diverse dishes. The owner, Shezzy, brings a unique touch to each item on the menu, elevating the familiar comfort foods to a new level. The pizza boasts a crispy crust and a medley of fresh, flavorful toppings, creating a symphony of tastes in every bite. The burgers are a testament to Shezzy's innovation, with succulent patties, premium ingredients, and a range of mouth-watering sauces that cater to diverse palates. The samosas, a classic snack, are crafted to perfection, featuring a golden, crispy exterior and a delectable filling that pays homage to traditional recipes. Despite a focused menu, Shezzy's commitment to quality and taste ensures that every customer leaves satisfied, having experienced a delightful culinary journey through these iconic offerings.
